Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 3, 2025

1. Who we are

This TikTok Video Uploader ("Application") is operated by Gared Berns ("we", "us"). If you have any questions about this policy, you can contact us at johnwalkerser@gmail.com.

2. What this application does

The Application allows you to upload video content to your TikTok account using TikTok’s official Content Posting API. The Application does not provide analytics, advertising, or any additional data processing beyond what is necessary to upload your videos.

3. Data we access via TikTok

Through TikTok’s APIs, we may access the following information:

  • your TikTok account ID (open_id or similar identifier);
  • your display name;
  • your profile avatar URL;
  • video files that you choose to upload through the Application.

We do not collect your TikTok password, email address, or other sensitive authentication information. Authentication is handled directly by TikTok.

4. How we use the data

The information listed above is used exclusively to:

  • authenticate your TikTok session via TikTok’s official login/authorization flow;
  • upload videos that you explicitly select to your TikTok account (for example, as drafts or posts);
  • display basic account information (such as display name or avatar) inside the Application interface, if applicable.

5. Data storage and retention

We do not permanently store personal data retrieved from TikTok on our own servers. Any data processed by the Application is used only for the duration of the upload and is discarded once the operation is complete, except where TikTok itself stores the content on its own platform in accordance with TikTok’s terms and policies.

We do not build long-term profiles of users, and we do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or resale.

6. Sharing of data with third parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties. Data may be processed by service providers that are strictly necessary to operate the Application (for example, hosting providers or TikTok itself), but only to the extent required to provide the core upload functionality.

7. Your choices and rights

  • You may revoke the Application’s access to your TikTok account at any time from your TikTok account settings.
  • If you no longer wish to use the Application, simply stop using it and, if applicable, remove its access via TikTok’s security/permissions page.
  • You can contact us at johnwalkerser@gmail.com if you have questions about how your data is processed in connection with this Application.

8. Children’s privacy

This Application is not directed to children under the age defined by applicable law in your jurisdiction. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children through this Application. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the Application or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the latest version came into effect.

Your continued use of the Application after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
