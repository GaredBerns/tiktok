Privacy Policy

This application allows users to upload video content to their TikTok accounts using TikTok’s official Content Posting API.

We do not store, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

The only information we access is:

  • user’s TikTok account ID
  • user’s display name
  • user’s avatar
  • video files that the user uploads

This information is used ONLY to:

  • Authenticate the user through TikTok Login Kit
  • Upload videos to the user’s TikTok account as drafts

We do not collect passwords, emails, or any sensitive information.

We do not store user data on our servers. All data is processed only during the upload process and then discarded.
